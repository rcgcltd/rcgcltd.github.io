about rcgc.ltd

Passionate indigenious entrepreneural startup firm with interest in civil, electrical projects and providing entrepreneural development training to the teeming youths of the South-Eastern part of Nigeria.
Established on 30 June 2015 and commenced full operations in the third quarter of 2015

Civil and Electrical Engineering
Entrepreneural Development

The company is a profit-oriented group, equipped and managed by high profile professionals in the manufacturing, construction, and communications industries with tremendous skills/ craftsmanship and reputation for undertaking quality services and projects in our field of operations. Other fields of our operation include: Agricultural Engineering and allied products, Communication and Media Production, Project Management and Rural Development
